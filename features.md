## üéÆ Game Mechanics & Difficulty

### 1. **Difficulty Levels**
   - **Easy**: 6 pairs, 15 tries
   - **Medium**: 8 pairs, 18 tries (current default)
   - **Hard**: 12 pairs, 20 tries
   - **Expert**: 16 pairs, 25 tries
   - Difficulty selector in settings
   - Make difficulty rises when the user wins in levels

### 2. **Timer/Stopwatch**
   - Elapsed time display
   - Best time tracking per difficulty
   - Time-based scoring system
   - Pause timer when game is paused

### 3. **Score System**
   - Points awarded for each match
   - Bonus points for speed
   - Penalty points for mismatches
   - Score multiplier for consecutive matches
   - High score tracking

### 4. **Streak Counter**
   - Track consecutive successful matches
   - Visual streak indicator
   - Streak bonus points
   - "On Fire" visual effect for high streaks
